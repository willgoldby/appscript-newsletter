About this add-on

This tool is designed specifically to help Google Shopping User Experience Researchers to quickly and easily create HTML-based emails for research findings.
If you're not on the Google Shopping UXR team or you are not trying to create an email for research findings, you can safely click the little X on the top right corner now.

How to use

  1. Open this template
  2. Make a copy
  3. Modify the contents without changing the "styles" (i.e., Font, Font size, Styles, etc.)
  4. Make sure your images are set to "in line" and as wide as the doc
  5. Change the name of the doc as it will be used as email subject
  6. Make sure you have updated the "full report" link
  7. Send to yourself by clicking the "send" button below
  8. Go to your inbox to check if everything looks good
  9. If something is wrong, don't hesitate to ping Ran
  10. If everything looks good, check the recipients below and/or add more by typing in the boxes.
  11. Click the "send" button and ta-da~~~
